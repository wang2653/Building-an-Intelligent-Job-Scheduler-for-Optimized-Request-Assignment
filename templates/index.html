Step 1 Login to the system

Click the Login button in the upper right corner of the home page. If you are a staff member at a nurse's station, you will be redirected to the Doctor Management interface; If you are a patient or a patient's family member, you will be redirected to the Patient Advisement interface.

Step 2: Add/Edit Patient Information

After logging in, you will be directed to the appropriate interface based on your role. If you are a patient or a patient's family member, you will be prompted to enter the required patient information.

Step 3: Check Doctor Status

You may want to check out Doctor Status page. Here, you can view real-time availability of doctors, including doctor's name, portrait, and workload.

Step 4: Check Patient Queuing Information

If you are a patient or patient's family member, the system will provide details of the patient queue after logging in. If any priority cases arise, the system will dynamically adjust the queue and notify affected patients.
